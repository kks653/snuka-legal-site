Delete Data

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Delete in the App

The fastest way to delete a photo session is inside Snuka Studio. Open the session and use the delete action. This removes reachable source photo rows, generated asset rows, and private storage objects for that session.

Email Request

If you cannot access the app, email privacy@snukastudio.com from an address where we can follow up. Include the approximate date of the session and any purchase receipt reference if the request involves paid full-resolution assets.

Automated Retention

Source portraits are eligible for cleanup 24 hours after a terminal generation or deletion state. Generated assets are eligible for cleanup after 90 days. Production cleanup runs on a schedule.

Limits

Some records may be retained when required for security, purchase accounting, dispute handling, or legal compliance. We do not use this retained data for advertising or tracking.
